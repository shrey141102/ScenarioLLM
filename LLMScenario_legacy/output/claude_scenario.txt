<scenario_description>
This enhanced scenario takes place on a 4-lane highway with bidirectional traffic. The scenario features a complex series of lane changes and speed adjustments that create multiple potential conflict points. The main challenge involves a chain reaction of emergency maneuvers triggered by a sudden slowdown of Vehicle 1 due to an obstacle in its lane. This forces Vehicle 3, which is following in the same lane, to execute an emergency lane change. Meanwhile, vehicles in adjacent lanes must react to these sudden maneuvers while maintaining their own trajectories. The scenario also includes a vehicle attempting to merge from an on-ramp, creating additional complexity. Several vehicles execute lane changes in close proximity to each other, creating a challenging prediction problem for an autonomous vehicle.
</scenario_description>
